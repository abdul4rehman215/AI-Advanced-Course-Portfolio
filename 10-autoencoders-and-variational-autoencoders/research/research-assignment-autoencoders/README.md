# Research Assignment — Autoencoders

This folder contains the autoencoders research assignment completed for the course.

## Research focus
This research work is centered on understanding autoencoders as an encoder-decoder framework, the main variants of autoencoders, their real-world use cases, and how architectural changes are made for different problem types.

## Main areas covered
- what an autoencoder is and what it learns
- encoder, latent space, bottleneck, and decoder concepts
- vanilla autoencoders
- denoising autoencoders
- convolutional autoencoders
- variational autoencoders (VAE)
- reconstruction loss and latent representations
- anomaly detection, denoising, compression, and feature learning use cases
- modular architectural modifications instead of fully replacing the full architecture
